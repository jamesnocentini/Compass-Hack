Research I/O

February 23-24, 2013 / London

About us

The world of Research meets Hackathons.

Many students in London have ideas and are thinking of new ways to solve everyday problems with technology. On the other hand, researchers are working on some amazing stuff but often the content is published in the academic field only. This event gives an opportunity to bring together students and researches to build something truly innovative.
